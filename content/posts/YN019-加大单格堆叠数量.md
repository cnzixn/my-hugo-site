```lua  

十九.加大单格堆叠数量	用记事本打开游戏目录/assets/DLC0002/scripts/tuning.lua文件，将以下内容：		STACK_SIZE_LARGEITEM = 10,		STACK_SIZE_MEDITEM = 20,		STACK_SIZE_SMALLITEM = 40,	替换为：		STACK_SIZE_LARGEITEM = 999,		STACK_SIZE_MEDITEM = 999,		STACK_SIZE_SMALLITEM = 999,	即可加大单格堆叠数量至999

```
