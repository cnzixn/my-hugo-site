二十五.伍迪砍树不会变海狸	用记事本打开游戏目录/assets/DLC0002/scripts/prefabs/woodie.lua文件，将inst.components.beaverness:DoDelta(3)替换为inst.components.beaverness:DoDelta(0)	即可让伍迪砍树不会变海狸
