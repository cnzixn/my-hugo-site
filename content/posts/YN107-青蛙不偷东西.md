```lua  

一０七.青蛙不偷东西	用记事本打开游戏目录/assets/scripts/prefabs/frog.lua文件，将inst.components.combat.onhitotherfn = function(inst, other, damage) inst.components.thief:StealItem(other) end替换为--inst.components.combat.onhitotherfn = function(inst, other, damage) inst.components.thief:StealItem(other) end	即可让青蛙在攻击主角时，主角不掉物品

```
